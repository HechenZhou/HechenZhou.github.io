About

Academic profile and research interests.

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Geotechnical Engineering at the Department of Geotechnical Engineering, College of Civil Engineering, Tongji University. I received my MSc in Geotechnical Engineering and my bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering from Tongji University.

My research combines soil mechanics, particulate micromechanics, constitutive modelling, and discrete element modelling. I am particularly interested in true triaxial stress states, static and dynamic small-strain stiffness, fabric anisotropy, and path-dependent behaviour of granular materials.
